Core Features

Simple, Yet Powerful

Ditch the cables, forget cloud accounts. LocalHub brings file transfer back to its most direct form.

Just a Browser

No app installation required for receivers. Access, download, and stream from any browser (Chrome, Safari, Edge).

Cross-Platform

Whether iOS, Android, Windows, Mac, or Linux, simply scan the QR code or enter the address to connect.

Privacy First

Data transfers only within your local network, never touching third-party cloud servers. Token-protected access for peace of mind.

Screen & Camera Stream

Share your screen or camera feed in real-time. Perfect for demonstrations, monitoring, or just sharing what you see.

Built for Every Scenario

LocalHub adapts to your needs flexibly

Classroom Sharing

Teachers can start LocalHub in class to share slides, PDFs, or videos via QR code. Students can download materials instantly on their phones or laptops without using internet data.

  • Supports simultaneous access for the whole class
  • Independent of campus network speed

Home Media Center

Want to watch movies or photos from your phone on a TV or tablet? Start LocalHub and stream directly to your TV's browser without complex casting protocols.

Office File Transfer

Need to share large files with colleagues? Bypass messaging app limits and slow speeds. Set up a temporary LocalHub file station for high-speed downloads to PC or Mac.
